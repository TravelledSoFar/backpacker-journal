Travel with heart

Must-know things before you travel

Travel is about gently entering someone else's home, culture and everyday life. We invite you to travel with curiosity, kindness and an open heart.

You are a traveller, not a critic

The food may be different, people may behave differently, and the language, habits and timings may not be what you are used to — and that is the beauty of travel.

Every place has its own gentle manners

We share guidelines on local customs, clothing and greetings — especially for religious or traditional spaces. Small gestures show care for the people who host us.

Be open to stepping out of your comfort zone

You might eat with your hands, sit on the floor, share a table with new people. Welcome new tastes, weather and levels of comfort as part of the experience.

Connect with places, do not just consume them

A few words in the local language, a warm smile, patient listening can create beautiful connections. We choose local businesses and women-led spaces.
